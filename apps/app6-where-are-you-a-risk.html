Where Are You a Risk?

A short seminar activity — about 12 minutes. Investors don't only ask if the idea is good — they ask how the founder and team are a risk. This helps you answer before they ask. It does not label you and it does not affect your marks.

Anonymous by default. No names required.

Before you start

This short activity is part of normal seminar learning. If you opt in, anonymous interaction data from your choices may be used to improve the teaching activity and nnherit's product design. Your participation or non-participation will not affect marks, feedback, or standing in the module. Do not enter your name or contact details. You can skip, participate without data capture, or withdraw later using the code shown at the end.

If you opt in, this activity will record which competence cards you place in each zone, your investor-concern rating, and your mitigation.

No problem

You can continue the seminar without the app. This is normal and fully fine. If you change your mind, reload this page.
