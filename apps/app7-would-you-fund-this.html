Would You Fund This?

A short seminar activity — about 15 minutes. It turns valuation from a guess into an evidence exercise — and then stress-tests your venture against a scenario you didn't choose. It does not label you and it does not affect your marks.

Anonymous by default. No names required.

Before you start

This short activity is part of normal seminar learning. If you opt in, anonymous interaction data from your choices may be used to improve the teaching activity and nnherit's product design. Your participation or non-participation will not affect marks, feedback, or standing in the module. Do not enter your name or contact details. You can skip, participate without data capture, or withdraw later using the code shown at the end.

If you opt in, this activity will record your strengths and risks with their grid placements and evidence tags, your confidence ratings, and your stress-test impact estimates alongside the cards' own scores.
