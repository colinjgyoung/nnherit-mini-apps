# Succession Dynamics — card content export for the nnherit mini apps

Extracted 2026-07-07 from the Drive sheet "nnherit_situation_cards_content_all.xlsx" (downloaded copy). Descriptions are the deck's own card copy. 60 cards, 12 per challenge. Card IDs and difficulty scores are in `Succession_Dynamics_Card_Checklist.csv` in the nnherit workspace; visuals in CARTES_NNHERIT.pdf.

Note: three situation names (Family Feud, Hidden Agendas, Shadow of Success) each exist as two distinct cards under different challenges — always match on situation + challenge.

In-app relabelling for the agnostic 12-card subset (deck untouched): Harmony → Team harmony, Family → Relationships; in descriptions, family member → team member and successor → team/leader where the app context is not family-business.
